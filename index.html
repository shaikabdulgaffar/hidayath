About App

Hidayate Amaal is a mobile-friendly web application designed to provide easy access to Islamic teachings and guidance. This app contains essential Islamic knowledge including beliefs, practices, and fundamental concepts of Islam.

Features:

  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Dark/Light mode support
  • Easy navigation and search
  • Offline reading capability
  • Bookmark important content

About Hidayate Amaal

Hidayate Amaal is a comprehensive Islamic guide that covers the fundamental beliefs and practices of Islam. It includes detailed explanations of:

  • Basic Islamic beliefs (Aqeedah)
  • The Five Pillars of Islam
  • Islamic calendar and important dates
  • Prophets and their teachings
  • Islamic terminology and concepts

This book serves as an essential reference for Muslims seeking to understand and practice their faith correctly.
