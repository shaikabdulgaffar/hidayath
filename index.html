About App

Hidayate Amaal is a mobile-friendly web application designed to provide easy access to Islamic teachings and guidance. This app contains essential Islamic knowledge including beliefs, practices, and fundamental concepts of Islam.

Features:

  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Dark/Light mode support
  • Easy navigation and search
  • Offline reading capability
  • Bookmark important content
  • Clean and intuitive interface
  • Fast and lightweight

How to Use:

  • Browse content from the main contents list
  • Use the search feature to find specific topics
  • Bookmark important content for quick access
  • Switch between light and dark modes
  • Share the app with friends and family

About Hidayate Amaal

Hidayate Amaal is a comprehensive Islamic guide that covers the fundamental beliefs and practices of Islam. This book serves as an essential reference for Muslims seeking to understand and practice their faith correctly.

What's Included:

  • Basic Islamic beliefs (Aqeedah)
  • The Five Pillars of Islam
  • Islamic calendar and important dates
  • Prophets and their teachings
  • Islamic terminology and concepts
  • Prayer methods and requirements
  • Fasting guidelines and rules
  • Purification and cleanliness
  • Islamic etiquettes and manners
  • Supplications and remembrance

Purpose:

This collection aims to provide Muslims with authentic Islamic knowledge in an easy-to-understand format. It covers both the theoretical aspects of faith and practical guidance for daily Islamic living.

Source:

The content is based on authentic Islamic sources including the Quran, Hadith, and classical Islamic scholarship, compiled to help Muslims strengthen their faith and practice.
